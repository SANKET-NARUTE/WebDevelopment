Fruit images and information is displayed here.

Fruits are the sweet and fleshy product of a tree or other plant that contains seed
and can be eaten as food. They are an essential source of vitamins, minerals,
and fiber that help maintain good health and prevent diseases.

Apple

Apples are one of the most popular and nutritious fruits in the world.
The saying "an apple a day keeps the doctor away" highlights their health benefits.
They come in various colors including red, green, and yellow.

Banana

Bananas are one of the most consumed fruits globally and a great energy source.
They are rich in potassium and help regulate blood pressure.
Bananas grow in tropical regions on large herbaceous flowering plants.

Strawberry

Strawberries are bright red, juicy, and sweet with a characteristic aroma.
They are packed with vitamin C and antioxidants.
Unlike most fruits, strawberries wear their seeds on the outside.
